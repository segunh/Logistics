Airport Protocol

VIP Meet and Greet

Our airport protocol services are available at International and Local airports, making your Airport's arrival /departure fast, safe, and pleasant on every journey to Nigeria. Our team helps expedite the issuance of a boarding pass with less stress. Travelers are accompanied through the boarding gate to the waiting lounge until the respective flights are boarding, then airborne.

    Departures

    • Receiving the clients at the departure reception desk.
    • Assisting with banking /acquiring foreign exchange.
    • Conducting check-in formalities with airlines, Immigration, and Customs on behalf of customers.
    • Escort to the executive waiting lounge
    • Escort to board aircraft

    Arrival

    • Receiving arriving clients at the arrivals halls
    • Assisting with banking/changing of foreign to local currency or cashing Traveler's cheques.
    • Assisting Passengers to locate check-in luggage from the carousel belt.
    • Provision of our Executive Bus for transfers, where applicable
    • Attending to client's needs till they leave.

Luggage Handling.

With our experience logistics officer right inside the baggage claim area, we ensure that our clients have a stress free process in claiming their bags, as we help to provide trolley in the hall and assist in baggage clearance with Customs, NDLEA and other security personnel before exiting the hall.

Lounge Services.

We understand the desires of our clients to have some rest before boarding the aircraft or at the arrival hall when they arrive, we have some dedicated lounge where you can feel relax before boarding. Our experience staff will be at the carousel belt to claim your bags in the arrival hall while you wait and relax in the lounge.

Custom Clearance and Immigration Assistance

International airports and immigration processes can be intimidating; especially for first-time guests to a country. Our licensed protocol team will meet passengers after exiting from the aircraft and assist them through the airport custom and immigration process and escort you through the system, ensuring a smooth and trouble-free passage until all documentation is completed. When exiting, our staff will ensure your exit is stress-free through baggage check-in, security checks.
