Who We Are?

Segdan Logistics was incorporated in 2019 to handle the business of Airport Protocol Services (VIP Meet and Greet, Luggage Handling and Services, Custom Clearance and Immigration Assistance), AIRLINE TRAVEL MANAGEMENT (Visa Assistance, Hotel Reservation), and LOGISTICS (Car Hire Services and Airport Pickup and Drop Off), Concession, Outsourcing, among others. We provide services for passengers to get maximum comfort while traveling, all services are designed and framed maintaining the standard of airlines and in accordance with IATA & ICAO regulations. We operates round the clock to meet our customers needs.
